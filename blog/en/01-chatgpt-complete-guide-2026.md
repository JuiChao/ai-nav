# ChatGPT Complete Guide: Latest Features, Pricing & Tips in 2026

Since its launch in November 2022, ChatGPT has evolved from an experimental chatbot into the world's largest AI platform. As of early 2026, its scale and influence have reached unprecedented heights. This guide provides a comprehensive breakdown based on the latest publicly available data.

## 1. ChatGPT by the Numbers in 2026

ChatGPT's growth trajectory has been remarkable. According to data from DemandSage, Panto, and other analytics platforms:

- **Weekly Active Users**: As of February–March 2026, ChatGPT reached **900 million** weekly active users (Source: [DemandSage](https://www.demandsage.com), [Panto](https://getpanto.ai))
- **Daily Prompts Processed**: The platform handles approximately **2.5 billion** prompts per day (Source: [WytLabs](https://wytlabs.com))
- **Monthly Visits**: Over **5 billion** monthly visits (Source: [FatJoe](https://fatjoe.com))
- **Paid Subscribers**: 50 million+ consumer subscribers and 9 million+ paying business users (Source: [Panto](https://getpanto.ai))

On the revenue front, OpenAI generates **$2 billion per month**, translating to an annualized revenue run rate exceeding **$25 billion**. In March–April 2026, OpenAI closed a funding round with $122 billion in committed capital, reaching a post-money valuation of **$852 billion** (Source: [Sacra](https://sacra.com)).

## 2. What Can ChatGPT Do Today?

After over three years of iteration, ChatGPT goes far beyond simple chat:

### Multimodal Understanding & Generation
ChatGPT accepts text, images, voice, and file inputs. You can photograph a math problem for it to solve, upload a PDF report for summarization, or have a voice conversation directly.

### Code Writing & Debugging
For developers, ChatGPT serves as a powerful coding assistant. It supports Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Go, and other major languages for writing, explaining, optimizing, and debugging code.

### Data Analysis
Upload CSV or Excel files, and ChatGPT can automatically analyze data, generate charts, and identify trends—particularly useful for non-technical professionals.

### Image Generation
With integrated DALL·E capabilities, you can generate and edit images directly within the conversation.

### Web Search
ChatGPT can search the internet in real-time for the latest information, no longer limited by its training data cutoff.

## 4. Five Tips for Using ChatGPT Effectively

### Tip 1: Provide Clear Context
Don't just say "write me an article." Instead, specify: "Write an 800-word technical blog comparing AI coding tools, targeting junior developers, in a professional yet accessible tone."

### Tip 2: Use Role Assignments
Starting your prompt with a role like "You are a senior data scientist" or "You are an SEO expert" significantly improves output quality.

### Tip 3: Break Tasks Into Steps
For complex tasks, divide them into multiple steps and confirm each before proceeding. This produces better results than requesting everything at once.

### Tip 4: Request Structured Output
Explicitly ask ChatGPT to format output as tables, lists, JSON, or Markdown for more organized results.

### Tip 5: Leverage Custom GPTs
OpenAI's GPT Store offers thousands of specialized GPTs—academic paper assistants, resume optimizers, code reviewers—that are more efficient than starting from scratch.
